    FAQs


    • How does UniSwift work? UniSwift is an integrated delivery platform accessible through an online website. Users can place orders for various items they need, and nearby students fulfill these delivery requests.
    • What types of deliveries does UniSwift offer? UniSwift offers three types of deliveries: intra-campus deliveries for 10 rupees, inter-campus deliveries for 20 rupees, and hometown deliveries for 200 rupees.
    • How does UniSwift ensure the safety and security of deliveries? UniSwift prioritizes safety by utilizing a personalized delivery network of nearby students. Additionally, all delivery transactions are monitored and tracked through the platform.
    • Can students earn money with UniSwift? Yes, students can earn income by fulfilling delivery requests through UniSwift's location-based notifications. They receive a portion of the delivery charge for each completed order.
    • Are there any restrictions on what UniSwift can deliver? UniSwift can deliver a wide range of items, including online order pickups, forgotten items, and items from students' hometowns. However, there may be restrictions on delivering certain prohibited items.
    • How does UniSwift handle payment for deliveries? UniSwift operates on a revenue-sharing model, where 50% of the delivery charge from each order contributes to the platform's revenue stream.
    • Can faculty members use UniSwift? Yes, UniSwift also caters to faculty members by delivering meals and supplies directly to their offices or classrooms, enhancing their work-life balance.
    • What are the benefits of using UniSwift? UniSwift offers time-saving convenience, economic opportunities for students, and enhanced accessibility to essential items for both students and faculty members.
    • How can I track my delivery status? Users can track their delivery status through the UniSwift website or mobile application, which provides real-time updates on the location of their items.
    • What sets UniSwift apart from other delivery services? UniSwift is tailored specifically for the Lovely Professional University campus, addressing the unique needs and challenges of its student and faculty population. It offers a personalized delivery network and a range of delivery options at affordable prices.
